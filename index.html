Shafer Stromwall

My name is Shafer Stromwall, and I am a senior music major at Luther College. I grew up in Minnetonka, MN, a SW suburb of Minneapolis. I enjoy playing trumpet and writing music, but I also enjoy playing all sorts of games with friends. I think my most important role at Luther is my position as part of the the Pep Band leadership, where I help maintain our music library. Next year's plan are still being planned, but I hope to start writing my own music for high school marching bands and pep bands.
